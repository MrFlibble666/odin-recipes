Chicken Curry

This is a really good recipe for spicy Indian chicken curry. It's pretty easy to make and tastes really good!

Ingredients

  • 800g skiless and bonless chicken thighs
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 inch ginger
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon coriander
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayane pepper
  • 1 teaspoon gram masala
  • 1 can chopped tomotoes
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • yoghurt to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in the pan on a medium heat and add the chopped onion.
  2. Cook until the onion is slightly translucant then add the garlic, ginger, curry powder, cumin, turmeric, coriander and cayanne.
  3. Stir the onion and spice mix for a couple of minutes then increase the heat and add the tomatoes.
  4. Once the tomatoes are boiling add the chicken thighs. Cover with a lid and simmer for 30 minutes
  5. Stir in the salt, pepper, gram massala and yoghurt.
  6. Enjoy! I recommend serving with plain basmati rice
